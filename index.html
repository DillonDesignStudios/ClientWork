Thoracoscopic rib plating system for minimally invasive fixation

AdvantageRib

AdvantageRib is a revolutionary alternative to traditional rib fixation systems.

AdvantageRib is FDA cleared for the fixation, stabilization, and fusion of rib fractures and osteotomies of normal and osteoporotic bone.

AdvantageRib Plating System consists of a pre-assembled stability plate with threaded locking posts, and locking caps inteded for the minimally invasive thoracoscopic fixation and stabilization of ribs. The implants are manufactured from titanium (CPTi4) and titanium alloy (Ti-6A1-7Nb).

About Us

SIG Medical is an emerging growth medical device company pioneering a minimally invasive Rib Fixation system that provides a clinical solution optimized for the operating room. Our mission is to provide more immediate and definitive patient care, reducing pneumonia, time in intensive care units, chronic pain, ventilator support, chest wall deformity, need for tracheostomy and significantly reduce the overall cost to the healthcare system.
